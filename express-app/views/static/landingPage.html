Bytesized Journal

A Journaling app for your Days and Nights

Bytesized Journal is a journaling web application in it's early alpha, designed to be personal, and private. Turn any device into a pocket journal organized with a calendar, no internet required.

Features

Calendar With all of you Journal Entries

See all of your journal entries laid out before you in a calendar. You can easily view how often you wrote down dreams or if you kept up with your daily journaling routine.

Journaling for Day and Night

You can write down your thoughts about the day and write down that incredible dream your don't want to forget into two dedicated sections.

Use on Any Device through the Web

Your device is your pocket journal. Any device that can open a web browser can use this app. It will load as a website and can be installed from their as an ordinary app.

Always on your Device

Bytesized Journal is opened as a website but saves your journal entries onto your own device. Your entries stay private to you, not even leaving your device, and are available without an internet connection. There is no signup form or email address required. All you need to do is hit the "get started" button and open the dashboard. The app will take care of everything else in the background.
